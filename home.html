Project Proposal

The project Luminary aims to create a well-trained ML model which is designed to optimize the learning-curve, minimize the instructor interaction and accelerate the self-learning process.

Project Specification Report

The combination of technology and pedagogy in modern education has resulted in creative paradigms, and the Luminary project is at the vanguard of this shift. Luminary is embarking on a quest to transform traditional educational approaches by incorporating AI-driven learning techniques and gamification components to empower high school students in their academic pursuits.

Analysis Report

The purpose of Analysis document is to outline the scope and requirements for the Luminary project, an educational platform designed to optimize the learning experience for high school students.

High-Level Design Report

The Luminary Project improves the learning process of a high school student with a personalized, AI-driven educational platform. The system will optimize the learning curve by identifying the weak areas in which the student might be having difficulties, thus generating targeted questions that will help the student channel more effort to his or her weak points in order to improve his or her grade.
